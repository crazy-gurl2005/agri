Featured Schemes

Scheme 1:Horticulture

This scheme aims to provide financial support to farmers through loans for horticulture purposes.

Scheme 2:Minor Irrigation

This scheme aims to provide financial assistance to farmers through loans intended for minor irrigation projects.

Scheme 3: Animal Husbandry

this scheme aims to provide financial support to farmers specifically for animal husbandry activitie.

Scheme 4:Tractor Purchase

This scheme aims to provide financial assistance for acquiring tractors, covering up to 90% of the tractor's cost.

Scheme 5: Sericulture In Irrigated Area.

This scheme aims to support farmers involved in sericulture within irrigated areas by offering loans ranging from ₹49,000/- to ₹79,000/- per acre.

Scheme 6:Seed Multiplication Scheme

This scheme offers subsidies to farmers to produce seeds under contract with the Department of Agriculture

Scheme 7:Rain Water Harvesting and Runoff Management Programme

This scheme aims to promote water harvesting and efficient use of rainwater in rural areas.

Scheme 8: NABARD assisted Rain Water Harvesting Programme

This scheme aims to promote rainwater harvesting and efficient water use in rural areas

Scheme 9: Minor Irrigation Scheme

This scheme aims to provide irrigation facilities to small and marginal farmers through construction of minor irrigation projects.

Scheme 10: Land Development Scheme area of operation

This scheme aims to bring barren and uncultivated land under cultivation, increasing agricultural productivity and farmers' income.

Scheme 11: World Bank-aided Tamil Nadu Irrigated Agricultural Modernization Project

This scheme aims to promote climate-resilient agriculture technologies, improve water management practices, and increase market opportunities for small and marginal farmers .

Scheme 12: The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

This scheme aims to enhance physical access of water on farms, expand cultivable area under assured irrigation, and improve on-farm water use efficiency .

Scheme 13:farm ponds to catch rainwater in farm lands:

This scheme aims to harvest and conserve rainwater for irrigation purposes, reducing dependence on groundwate.

Scheme 14:providing electric motor pumpsets to farmers with subsidy

This scheme aims to promote efficient irrigation practices by providing subsidized electric motor pumpsets to farmers.

Scheme 15: the Land Development scheme

This scheme to improve the productivity of land by providing financial assistance to farmers for land development activities.

Scheme 16: the Sub Mission on Agricultural Mechanization

This scheme aims to promote mechanization in agriculture, increasing efficiency and productivity

Scheme 17: Establishment of Sugarcane-Based Custom Hiring Centers

This scheme aims to promote mechanization in sugarcane farming, reducing labor costs and increasing efficiency.

Scheme 18: Chief Minister's Scheme of Solar Powered Pumpsets

This scheme aims to provide standalone solar-powered pumpsets to farmers with a 70% subsidy.

Scheme 19: Installation of Solar Fencing Units schemes

This scheme aims to protect crops from wild animals and stray cattle using solar-powered fencing

Scheme 20: The National Food Security Mission Pulses

This scheme aims to increase pulse production and productivity, enhancing food security and farmers' income.

Scheme 21: The NADP - Paddy scheme

This scheme aims to increase paddy production and productivity by providing subsidies to farmers for machine transplanting.

Scheme 22: (NFSM) Rice scheme India.

This scheme aims to increase rice production and productivity through sustainable practices.
